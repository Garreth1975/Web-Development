The Band Allen

The Band Allen Started back in 2018, It came about when Garreth Allen was asked to play
in Church for Offertory one Sabbath. After practicing the Song his Daughter who plays the
Double bass decided to join in. The practice session was going great, then his son Storm decided
to join in as well, what ensued was just a great sound of music that made everyone excited
Later on Son Ocean Joined in with his Violin. Thus started the Band Allen.
